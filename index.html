About Me

From information technology to jewelry The path from information technology to jewelry is not one I think is a well paved one but I found it and don't plan on leaving. I am primarily a self taught jewelry artist and enjoy exploring different jewelry making and gemstone setting techniques. So, you will continue to see my jewelry evolve over time. I save sterling silver scraps from my workbench and recycle the metal by melting it and rolling it out into sheet metal of my desired gauge. The metal is then cut, hammered, filed and otherwise shaped or "reborn" into new jewelry components.
